# PD Rules

Law enforcement officers are expected to maintain professional, realistic, and high-quality roleplay at all times. Officers represent one of the core departments within Brick City RP and are expected to create fair and enjoyable interactions for the entire server.

Police officers must follow proper escalation and realistic procedures during all situations. Officers may not rush scenes, force outcomes, or escalate situations unnecessarily simply to secure arrests or "win" interactions. The goal of law enforcement roleplay is to create believable and balanced scenarios, not end scenes as quickly as possible.

Abuse of police authority, bias, favoritism, corruption outside approved roleplay, or intentionally targeting players without proper reasoning is strictly prohibited. Officers are expected to remain fair and professional during all interactions regardless of personal feelings or affiliations.

Unrealistic policing, unnecessary use of force, excessive aggression, baiting players into conflict, or intentionally seeking shootouts is not allowed. Officers are expected to value their lives and the lives of civilians during dangerous situations.

Police officers may not leak confidential information, abuse MDT systems, misuse departmental equipment, or use their position for personal gain. Any abuse of departmental tools or permissions may result in immediate disciplinary action.

Professionalism is expected both in-character and out-of-character. Toxic behavior, low-effort roleplay, trolling, or conduct that negatively impacts the department or server environment will not be tolerated.

Law enforcement positions are privileges, not guaranteed roles. Failure to meet department expectations, repeated inactivity, poor professionalism, unrealistic conduct, abuse of authority, or violations of server rules may result in strikes, suspension, demotion, blacklist, or permanent termination from the department.
