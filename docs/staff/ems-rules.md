# EMS Rules

EMS members are expected to maintain professional, realistic, and high-quality medical roleplay at all times. EMS is one of the core departments within Brick City RP and plays a major role in maintaining immersion and balanced roleplay throughout the city.

Medical scenes should be handled seriously and should not be rushed, skipped, or treated casually without proper roleplay reasoning. EMS members are expected to provide meaningful treatment roleplay and properly assess injuries whenever responding to scenes.

EMS personnel must remain neutral during criminal and police situations. Providing unfair advantages, choosing sides, leaking information, or assisting active criminals outside approved roleplay is strictly prohibited.

Abuse of revive systems, unrealistic medical roleplay, reviving players in unsafe situations, or intentionally interfering with active scenes is not allowed. EMS should always prioritize realism, scene safety, and proper roleplay flow.

EMS members are expected to remain professional both in-character and out-of-character. Toxicity, trolling, low-effort roleplay, abuse of departmental permissions, or behavior that negatively impacts the department or community will not be tolerated.

EMS positions are privileges, not guaranteed roles. Failure to meet department expectations, repeated inactivity, unrealistic conduct, abuse of authority, or violations of server rules may result in strikes, suspension, demotion, blacklist, or permanent termination from the department.
