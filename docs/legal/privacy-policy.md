This information is used strictly for moderation, security, anti-cheat, whitelist systems, support, economy tracking, and overall server functionality.

Any information collected is stored only as long as necessary for server operations, moderation, support, or legal/security purposes.

By joining or interacting with Brick City RP services, you consent to the collection and use of this information for community and operational purposes.

If you do not agree with this policy, you should discontinue use of Brick City RP services.
