# Green Zone Conduct

Green Zones are designated safe areas where hostile or criminal roleplay is prohibited. These areas are intended to protect important public locations and allow players to access services without unnecessary disruption.

## Prohibited Actions

The following actions are not allowed inside Green Zones:

- Shooting or violent roleplay
- Robberies or kidnappings
- Scamming or baiting players
- Vehicle ramming or reckless driving
- Hostile initiation or escalation
- Stashing weapons or using Green Zones to avoid active roleplay

Players may not enter a Green Zone to escape an active situation, avoid consequences, or abuse safe zone protections. Doing so may be considered Powergaming or FailRP.

If a roleplay scene begins outside of a Green Zone and naturally moves into one, staff may treat the area as a temporary grey zone depending on the situation. However, players are still expected to keep scenes realistic and controlled.

Businesses, hospitals, government buildings, garages, spawn areas, and server event locations are all considered protected areas unless stated otherwise by staff.

Using Green Zones to troll, provoke reactions, or interfere with ongoing roleplay is prohibited. Repeated violations of Green Zone rules may result in severe punishment depending on the situation.
