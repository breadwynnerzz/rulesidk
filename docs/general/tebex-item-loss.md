# Tebex Purchase Policy

## Item Loss

Brick City RP is not responsible for item loss caused by player mistakes, unsupported transfers, accidental drops, gameplay situations, or server wipes.

If an issue happens because of a confirmed server-side problem, we'll do our best to review it fairly.

---

## Refund & Replacement Eligibility

Refunds or item replacements may be considered if:

- Clear evidence is provided. Video or screenshots help a lot.
- The issue was directly caused by a verified server problem.
- The report is made within 24 hours of the incident.

---

## Not Eligible For Refunds/Replacements

The following situations are generally not eligible:

- Accidentally dropping or deleting items
- Giving items to the wrong player
- Vehicle loss caused by improper storage
- Scams, robberies, or RP-related situations
- Punishments resulting in item wipes

---

## Important


:::danger Chargebacks or unauthorized payment disputes may result in a permanent ban from Brick City RP services and platforms.
