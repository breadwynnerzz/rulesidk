# General Rules

## 1. Community Conduct

All players are expected to treat others with respect at all times. Harassment, discrimination, toxicity, hate speech, or real-life threats toward players or staff will not be tolerated anywhere within the community.

## 2. Streamer Friendly Community

Brick City RP is a streamer-friendly 18+ community. All players are expected to follow both Twitch Terms of Service and Discord Terms of Service at all times while participating in the server or community platforms.

- [Twitch Terms of Service](https://www.twitch.tv/p/en/legal/terms-of-service/)
- [Discord Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms)

## 3. Microphone & Clipping Requirements

Players must have a working microphone with push-to-talk enabled at all times. All players are also required to have clipping software installed and must be able to provide POV footage when requested by staff.

## 4. Out of Character (OOC)

Breaking character during active roleplay is prohibited. Discussing reports, punishments, Discord conversations, or other out-of-character topics mid-scene is not allowed.

## 5. Metagaming

Using information gained outside of roleplay for in-character advantage is strictly prohibited. This includes information from Discord, Twitch streams, screen sharing, or outside communication.

## 6. Powergaming

Powergaming is the abuse of mechanics or unrealistic actions to gain an advantage during roleplay. Players are expected to keep interactions realistic and fair at all times.

## 7. Safe Zones

Criminal activity is prohibited inside designated Safe Zones. This includes robbing, kidnapping, shooting, or initiating hostile roleplay. Active scenes that enter a Safe Zone may be treated as grey-zone situations at staff discretion.

## 8. Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Attacking, killing, or engaging in combat without proper roleplay initiation is not allowed. All hostile interactions must contain valid and realistic roleplay beforehand.

## 9. Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Using vehicles as weapons to intentionally injure, kill, or disable players or vehicles without valid roleplay is prohibited.

## 10. Value of Life & FailRP

Players are expected to realistically value their character's life during dangerous situations. Unrealistic actions, refusing to fear weapons, continuing to fight while incapacitated, or ignoring realistic consequences may be considered FailRP.

## 11. Criminal Roleplay

All criminal roleplay must contain meaningful interaction and realistic reasoning. Random robberies, "lootboxing," or low-effort roleplay intended only for profit are not allowed.

## 12. Police & Emergency Services

Players are expected to provide quality roleplay during interactions with law enforcement and EMS. Unrealistic police chases, shooting police without proper roleplay, or interfering with active scenes unrealistically is prohibited.

## 13. Combat Logging & Exploiting

Disconnecting during active roleplay to avoid consequences is strictly prohibited. Exploiting, glitching, duplicating items, or abusing game mechanics for advantage is not allowed.

## 14. Character Standards

Characters must have realistic names and believable personalities that fit the serious roleplay environment. Troll names, parody characters, or unrealistic personas are not permitted.

## 15. Community & Discord Rules

All Discord channels and community spaces must remain respectful and drama-free. Advertising other servers, staff harassment, ban evasion, or leaking personal information will result in severe punishment.

## 16. Rule Enforcement

Staff reserve the right to enforce server rules as needed to maintain a fair and enjoyable roleplay environment. Failure to follow server rules may result in warnings, character kills, suspensions, whitelist removal, or permanent bans depending on the severity of the violation.
