# Reporting Requirements

All players are required to have clipping software installed and functional while playing on Brick City RP. Players must be able to provide POV footage if requested by staff during reports, investigations, or administrative situations.

All reports must include clear, unedited video evidence showing the full situation from beginning to end. Clips must contain enough context for staff to properly review the scene, including audio and all involved parties. A minimum of three minutes is required unless staff requests additional footage.

Reports must be submitted within 24 hours of the incident. Reports submitted late, lacking context, heavily edited, or missing evidence may be denied without review.

:::warning The report system is intended to resolve legitimate rule violations, not personal disputes or revenge reports.

False reports, misleading clips, withholding context, or abusing the report system to target other players may result in punishment.

When submitting a report, remain respectful and keep all information clear, factual, and straight to the point.
