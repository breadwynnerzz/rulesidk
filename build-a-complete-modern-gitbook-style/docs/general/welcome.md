# Welcome To Brick City

Welcome to Brick City RP. Before playing, make sure you read and understand all server rules and expectations. Our goal is to provide a serious, realistic, and enjoyable roleplay experience for everyone in the community.

This rulebook contains all server guidelines, roleplay expectations, department rules, and community policies. All players are expected to stay updated with any changes made to these rules.

## What We Expect

- Respect all players and staff
- Remain in character during roleplay
- Use common sense at all times
- No exploiting, trolling, or toxic behavior
- Value realistic and immersive roleplay

:::warning Failure to follow server rules may result in warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans depending on the severity of the situation.

Not knowing the rules does not excuse rule violations.

Thank you for being part of the Brick City RP community. We look forward to the stories and experiences you create throughout the city.
