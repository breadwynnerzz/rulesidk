# Weazel News

Weazel News members are expected to provide professional, realistic, and high-quality media roleplay at all times. As a public-facing department, members are expected to represent the server and community in a mature and respectful manner.

News roleplay should focus on creating immersive city coverage, interviews, event reporting, and media-related content that contributes positively to the server environment. Low-effort trolling, baiting reactions, or intentionally disrupting active scenes for content is not allowed.

Weazel News personnel are expected to remain neutral during active situations. Members may not interfere with police scenes, criminal situations, negotiations, or ongoing investigations unless authorized through roleplay.

Abuse of media access, leaking sensitive information, stream-sniping, or using Weazel News status to gain unfair advantages is strictly prohibited. Members may not use their role to avoid consequences or force themselves into scenes where they are not realistically involved.

Professionalism is expected both in-character and out-of-character. Toxicity, unrealistic conduct, abuse of permissions, or behavior that negatively impacts the department or server environment may result in disciplinary action.

Weazel News positions are privileges, not guaranteed roles. Failure to meet department expectations, repeated inactivity, poor professionalism, or violations of server rules may result in strikes, suspension, demotion, blacklist, or permanent termination from the department.
