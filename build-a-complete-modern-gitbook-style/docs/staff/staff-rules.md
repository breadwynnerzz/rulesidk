# Staff Rules

:::info Placeholder page. Add official internal staff rules, moderation procedures, and escalation policies here.

Staff members are expected to enforce rules consistently, remain professional, avoid conflicts of interest, and prioritize the long-term health of the community.

## Staff Expectations

- Treat players with respect during every interaction
- Review evidence carefully before issuing punishments
- Avoid handling reports involving close friends, enemies, or personal conflict
- Keep staff-only information confidential
- Document moderation actions clearly
- Escalate serious issues to senior staff

## Evidence Standards

Staff should rely on clear clips, logs, screenshots, and consistent witness information whenever possible. When evidence is incomplete, staff should avoid assumptions and request additional context.
