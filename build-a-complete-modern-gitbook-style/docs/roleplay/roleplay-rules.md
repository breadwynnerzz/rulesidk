# Roleplay Rules

Brick City RP is a serious roleplay server. All players are expected to maintain realistic, immersive, and story-driven roleplay at all times. The goal of every interaction should be to create enjoyable and believable scenarios for everyone involved, not simply to "win" situations.

Players must remain in character during active roleplay scenes unless instructed otherwise by staff. Breaking character, using out-of-character information, or intentionally disrupting scenes damages immersion and may result in punishment.

Low-effort roleplay, trolling, unrealistic behavior, or treating the server like GTA Online is not allowed. Every action your character takes should make sense within the situation and reflect realistic decision-making.

Players are expected to value their character's life and the lives of others during dangerous situations. Refusing to comply during valid roleplay scenarios, repeatedly escalating situations unrealistically, or showing no fear during life-threatening encounters may be considered FailRP.

Randomly targeting players, creating scenes solely for violence, or starting hostile interactions without proper roleplay is prohibited. Criminal roleplay should contain meaningful interaction, proper escalation, and realistic motives behind actions.

If a situation becomes heated or confusing, players are expected to finish the roleplay scene before handling issues through reports or staff assistance. Arguing, threatening reports, or breaking character mid-scene is not allowed.

:::info Use common sense at all times. Not every scenario can be covered directly within the rules, and staff reserve the right to punish behavior that negatively affects the server's roleplay environment even if it is not specifically listed here.
