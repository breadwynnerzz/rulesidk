BRICK CITY RP SERVER RULES

Please review ALL RULES thoroughly before joining the city. These rules are in place to ensure fair, immersive, and enjoyable roleplay for everyone.

We HIGHLY RECOMMEND having clipping software enabled at all times, as staff decisions are primarily based on evidence and recorded interactions.

NO CLIP = NO SIT.

General Rules

Clipping System / Reports / Tickets

  1. Players are strongly encouraged to use clipping software such as NVIDIA, AMD, Medal, or similar software. Reports without evidence are INVALID.
  2. Any clip submitted for a report should generally include at least two minutes of footage and clearly identify the involved player(s).
  3. Reports using video evidence must contain all relevant audio and context surrounding the incident.
  4. Retaliatory or revenge reporting is PROHIBITED. Reports should be submitted within 30 minutes of the incident whenever possible.
  5. Rule breaks do not automatically end roleplay scenes. Finish the scene whenever reasonable and report the incident afterward.
  6. Repeatedly creating reports or abusing the ticket system may result in reports being ignored or disciplinary action.
  7. Anti-cheat bans may be reviewed by management. Account history and prior detections may be considered during appeals.
  8. Verification teams reserve the right to deny verification requests that cannot be reasonably validated.

Main Rules

Main Rules

  1. All community members are expected to treat others respectfully. All members of Brick City RP must be 18 years of age or older. Anyone found under 18 will be permanently removed.
  2. Arguing with staff rulings during active situations is PROHIBITED.
  3. Purposely going AFK to avoid roleplay situations is not allowed.
  4. Using macros, keybinds, or exploits to gain an unfair advantage is PROHIBITED.
  5. Bugs and exploits should be reported immediately and may not be abused for personal gain.
  6. Criminal activities are restricted to 15 minutes before server restarts or events.

RDM / VDM / NLR

Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Definition: Random Deathmatch occurs when a player injures or kills another player without sufficient roleplay or proper initiation.

  1. You must have a valid reason and must threaten the victim's life before killing. Scenes must be at least 2 minutes or longer before engaging in violence.
  2. Both parties MUST have two negative interactions before any killing is involved.
  3. Players must establish meaningful roleplay before using lethal force.

Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Definition: Vehicle Deathmatch occurs when a player intentionally uses a vehicle to injure or kill another player.

  1. Purposely ramming another player or using vehicles as weapons is prohibited.
  2. A player aiming a gun at you while standing in front of the vehicle and you decide to drive forward and hit them is considered VDM. Someone standing in the road is not considered VDM, but be mindful.

New Life Rule (NLR)

Definition: When your character dies, that character may not remember the events directly surrounding their death.

  1. Players may not immediately return to the location of their death.
  2. Information surrounding your death cannot be used for revenge purposes.
  3. Returning solely to continue the same conflict is prohibited.

Fear RP / Valuing Life / Fail RP

Fear RP / Fail RP

Fear RP Definition: You must value your character's life and react reasonably during dangerous situations.

Fail RP Definition: Performing unrealistic, impossible, or immersion-breaking actions.

  1. Players should reasonably comply when clearly outnumbered or overpowered.
  2. Characters are expected to react realistically to threats involving weapons or severe danger.
  3. Ignoring obvious threats to continue fighting may constitute Fail RP.
  4. If a player is attempting auto theft, the owner has the right to use lethal force after 2 verbal warnings.
  5. In a 1v1 situation, you may defend yourself by all means if your weapon is already withdrawn. If not, you must fear for your life.
  6. In a 1v2 situation, you must fear for your life unless your weapon is already withdrawn.
  7. Having your weapon out only applies to 1v1 and 1v2 situations, not anything more.
  8. If you are outnumbered by 3 or more people, you MUST fear for your life. Failure to do so will result in Fail RP along with you giving up your pockets.
  9. If both parties have an equal amount to each other, it is free game. Die trying.
  10. If PD or EMS arrive after a shootout has ended, all remaining involved survivors must leave the scene immediately.
  11. Players MUST fear the police.
  12. Players are not allowed to rob, punch, kidnap, shoot, threaten, or kill players on a crutch or wheelchair unless they bring it upon themselves.
  13. Players on a crutch or wheelchair lose this protection if actively trapping or giving callouts over the radio.
  14. Handcuffed players may not operate any motor vehicle, bike, or radio. This is Power Gaming and FRP.

Metagaming / Powergaming

Metagaming

Definition: Bringing information learned outside of roleplay into roleplay.

  • Discord communications
  • Streams
  • Screen shares
  • Voice calls
  • External chats

Information learned outside of roleplay may not be used to gain advantages in character.

Powergaming

Definition: Performing actions that would be impossible, unrealistic, or not reasonably achievable.

  • Providing locations while incapacitated
  • Entering protected areas solely to avoid consequences
  • Selling illegal items inside protected zones
  • Operating vehicles while restrained
  • Unrealistic movement or actions
  • Selling drugs on mountains, tunnels, and similar locations

Robbing / Scamming / Logging / Exploits

Robbing & Scamming

Robbing Definition: Taking another player's belongings through threats or force.

Scamming Definition: Deceiving another player during exchanges or transactions.

Kill OR rob city: You may kill a player or rob a player during an interaction, but not both. If you choose to kill, do not rob. If you choose to rob, do not kill.

  1. Robberies require at least two meaningful roleplay interactions.
  2. Players should have legitimate roleplay reasoning before initiating robberies.
  3. Fighting, robbing, and killing players all within the same interaction without proper escalation is prohibited.
  4. Compliant victims may not simply be executed.
  5. Players may not force others to sell assets, withdraw money, or produce items not present during the scene.
  6. Robberies involving the same players should be reasonably spaced apart.
  7. Scamming server-owned assets, administrative items, or prohibited items is not allowed.
  8. Brick City is not liable for transactions that take place outside of the city.

Asset Transferring

Definition: Transferring items, money, vehicles, or assets between your own characters without authorization.

Asset transferring for unfair progression or bypassing server mechanics is STRICTLY PROHIBITED.

Combat Logging

  1. Combat logging will result in disciplinary action and possible inventory loss.
  2. Players unintentionally disconnected should return to roleplay as soon as possible.
  3. Combat logging on police at any time will result in IMMEDIATE BAN.

Exploiting

Definition: Abusing game mechanics, bugs, or unintended features for advantages.

  • Duplication glitches
  • Clipping through objects
  • Abuse of emotes
  • Fake NPC interactions
  • Economy exploits
  • Modding / Mod Menus / Triggers

Greenzones / Third-Party Mods

Greenzones

Police stations, hospitals, vehicle garages, and businesses are all GREENZONES.

Definition: Protected areas where criminal activities are prohibited.

  • Hospital
  • Vehicle Garages
  • City Hall
  • Interior Banks
  • Vehicle Dealerships
  • Designated Businesses
  • Administrative Event Areas
  • Casino except parking lot
  • Player Owned Businesses
  • Tattoo Shop
  • Hair Shop
  • Clothing Store
  • Moneywash

If a roleplay scene begins outside of a Greenzone and unintentionally moves into one, staff may review the situation based on context.

Third-Party Modifications

Definition: Any software or modification providing unfair advantages.

  • Cheats
  • Tracers
  • ESP
  • Aimbots
  • Exploit menus
  • No Props

The use of unauthorized modifications will result in PERMANENT BAN.

Faction Association Rules

Faction Association Rules

  1. Official faction members must properly represent their organizations.
  2. Faction clothing, identifiers, and accessories should be worn at ALL TIMES.
  3. Factions should have at least 2 negative roleplay interactions before initiating conflicts.
  4. These interactions must have video and audio clipped.
  5. Civilians entering active faction territories assume the risks associated with dangerous areas.
  6. Factions may not target random civilians without sufficient roleplay reasoning.

Government Job Rules

Roleplaying With PD & EMS

  1. Only authorized employees may operate government vehicles.
  2. Players cannot take weapons or equipment other than radios and cellphones from police.
  3. Kidnapping government employees requires meaningful roleplay, but kidnapping EMS workers is STRICTLY PROHIBITED.
  4. Players revived by EMS should continue roleplay realistically. Avoiding a crutch or wheelchair is prohibited.
  5. Government impersonation is prohibited.
  6. Do not interfere with active police scenes with lethal weapons. If they are chasing your friend, you cannot just shoot them; only the person actually being chased can shoot, and they cannot shoot first. Tasers do not count.

Police & EMS Conduct

  1. Government employees may not abuse powers, privileges, or resources.
  2. Government employees may not participate in illegal activity while actively serving in official positions.
  3. EMS should avoid entering active shootouts until scenes are secure.
  4. Government-issued equipment may not be distributed to unauthorized players. This is a BANNABLE OFFENSE.
  5. Players physically seen shooting are eligible to be searched for illegal items and weapons. Illegal items will be confiscated and subject to arrest.
  6. Cop baiting is prohibited.
  7. VIP weapons are subject to seizure like any other weapon unless you have the VIP Weapon License on you at the time of search and seizure.

Microphone Rules

Players MUST have a functioning microphone free of excessive echo, static, or disruptive noise.

OOC / TOS / Other

Out of Character (OOC)

  1. Players should remain in character at ALL TIMES.
  2. OOC chat may not be used for insults, harassment, or arguments.
  3. Toxic behavior on any platform connected to Brick City RP may result in removal from the community.
  4. Character names must remain realistic and comply with server guidelines.

Terms of Service (TOS)

By joining Brick City RP, players agree to follow all server rules and community standards.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, leaking personal information, impersonation of staff, hate speech, cheating, or severe toxicity may result in removal from the community. Any of these violations will result in a permanent ban.

  3. Players may roleplay as any gender as long as it is done respectfully and realistically. Trolling, harassment, deception, or disruptive use of voice changers is prohibited.
